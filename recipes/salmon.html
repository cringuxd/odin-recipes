Simple recipe for salmon cooked in garlic butter!

  • Salmon fillets
  • Garlic
  • Butter
  • Lemon
  • Parsley
  1. Season salmon with salt and pepper.
  2. Sear the curved presentation side of the salmon and cook for 3 minutes.
  3. Turn the salmon, cook another minute, then add the butter. As soon as the butter melts, add the garlic.
  4. Start basting immediately after you've added the garlic, right before it turns golden. Continue for 1.5 minutes
  5. Rest for 3 minutes, then serve!
